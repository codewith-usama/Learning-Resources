Data analyst life cycle

1. Planning (Which kind of data it's need, how it's gonna manage throughout it's life cycle, who will
	be resposible for it and the optimal outcomes)

2. Capture (Collecting data from different organization and brought into organiztion)

3. Manage (Care for data, how and where to store, the tool to keep it save and secure and the action
	taken to make sure that it maintains properly) This phase is very important for data cleaning

4. Analyze (data is used to solve problems, make decision and support business goals)

5. Archive (it data is not used for a longer time make it Archive rather than to take it around)

6. Destroy (Important to protect company's private information as well as private data from customers)
